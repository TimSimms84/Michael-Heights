Welcome to Michael Heights Neighborhood Association

Located in the heart of Tulsa, Oklahoma, Michael Heights is a vibrant community known for its friendly residents, picturesque streets, and a rich history that dates back decades. Our neighborhood association is dedicated to ensuring that Michael Heights remains a safe, welcoming, and thriving place for all its residents.

Community Highlights

  • Beautiful parks and green spaces perfect for family outings and picnics.
  • A strong sense of community with annual events, block parties, and neighborhood meetings.
  • Proximity to top-rated schools, shopping centers, and local attractions in Tulsa.

Join Us!

If you're a resident of Michael Heights and want to get involved, consider joining the neighborhood association. Together, we can make Michael Heights an even better place to live!

About Michael Heights Neighborhood Association

The Michael Heights Neighborhood Association is a volunteer-driven organization committed to enhancing the quality of life in the Michael Heights neighborhood in Tulsa. Our mission is to foster a strong sense of community, ensure the safety and well-being of all residents, and serve as a resource for neighborhood improvement and social connection.

Our Mission

To build a vibrant, inclusive, and safe community through active engagement, advocacy, and collaboration.

Emergency Preparedness

Being prepared for emergencies is crucial for the safety and well-being of our community. The Michael Heights Neighborhood Association is committed to providing resources and information to help residents prepare for various types of emergencies.


Essential Emergency Checklist

  • Keep a first aid kit in an easily accessible location.
  • Store at least a three-day supply of non-perishable food and water.
  • Have a flashlight and extra batteries handy.
  • Keep important documents in a fireproof and waterproof container.
  • Know the emergency evacuation routes and have a family meeting point.

Helpful Emergency Resources

  • FEMA's Guide to Emergency Preparedness
  • Local Emergency Services Contact Information
  • CDC's Emergency Health Tips
  • Red Cross Emergency Checklist

Contact for Emergency Support

If you have questions or need assistance with emergency preparedness, please reach out to our Emergency Preparedness Committee. We're here to help ensure that every resident is prepared and informed.
