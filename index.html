Welcome to Michael Heights Neighborhood Association

Located in the heart of Tulsa, Oklahoma, Michael Heights is a vibrant community known for its friendly residents, picturesque streets, and a rich history that dates back decades. Our neighborhood association is dedicated to ensuring that Michael Heights remains a safe, welcoming, and thriving place for all its residents.

Community Highlights

  • Beautiful parks and green spaces perfect for family outings and picnics.
  • A strong sense of community with annual events, block parties, and neighborhood meetings.
  • Proximity to top-rated schools, shopping centers, and local attractions in Tulsa.

Join Us!

If you're a resident of Michael Heights and want to get involved, consider joining the neighborhood association. Together, we can make Michael Heights an even better place to live!
